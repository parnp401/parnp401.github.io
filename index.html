Installation in a public space with JOE CHUNG // CHERRY HILLS x CENTRAL PARK

//PLACE
Cherry Hills is part of New York's famous Central Park and is located on the Upper Western part of the park.
The place is known for its romantic water fountain and for the great photo spots towards the castle.
It had always been an important location for horse carriages too, because the fountain offers an accessible source of water for the horses.


//MAPPING
As Cherry Hills is a circle, we were interested in the behaviour of visitors, horses, birds and dogs around.


//INSTALLATION x PROTOTYPE
Ideation is still an ongoing process, but research helped us to shape a couple of outstanding ideas.
